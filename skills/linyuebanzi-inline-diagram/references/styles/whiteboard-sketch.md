# 风格: 白板手绘风 (whiteboard-sketch)

**适用场景**: 技术教程、概念讲解、流程说明、架构入门。传达"一位 skilled illustrator 在白板上随手画的教育图示"的感觉——亲切、清晰、有温度。

## 风格基因(每个提示词开头必须包含)

```
Style: Hand-drawn whiteboard sketch diagram, like a skilled illustrator drawing on a whiteboard to explain a concept. Black ink marker strokes with organic, slightly wavy and imperfect lines. Soft rounded rectangular containers with pastel fills. Simple minimalist stick figure characters with round heads.

Background: warm cream off-white (#F5F0E8 to #FAF6EE), solid flat color, no grid lines, no paper texture, no grain.

Lines and strokes:
- Black ink hand-drawn outlines with visible organic variation (slightly wobbly, never perfectly straight or geometric)
- Container outlines: medium weight, slightly blobby at corners
- Connectors and arrows: thinner strokes, hand-drawn arrowheads
- Rounded corners on all containers, hand-sketched not geometrically precise

Color palette (soft pastel, low saturation):
- Mint/sage green: positive elements, completed states, correct answers
- Soft blue: informational, neutral, process steps
- Warm peach/coral: attention items, contrasts, highlights
- Soft lavender/purple: secondary categories, structure elements
- Light yellow: highlight zones, callout backgrounds
- Each color serves a functional purpose, not decorative

Typography:
- Clean sans-serif for main labels and titles
- Hand-lettered style for small annotations near arrows and margins
- Large bold Chinese headline at top
- Short Chinese labels (2-6 characters) preferred for cards and nodes
- English only for established technical terms (e.g. API, Agent, LLM)

Characters:
- Simple minimalist stick figures with round heads and minimal bodies
- Used sparingly as narrative helpers (thinking, reacting, pointing)
- Speech or thought bubbles for character expressions
- Characters should not dominate the composition

Icons: hand-drawn doodle-style mini illustrations (magnifying glass, database, document, gear, etc.), sketched quality matching the line style.

Layout: generous whitespace, clear visual hierarchy, educational diagram feel, flat 2D. Arrows connect elements with thin hand-drawn lines. Bottom summary sentence in bold handwritten Chinese style.

Do not use photographs, 3D rendering, glossy effects, heavy shadows, dark backgrounds, neon colors, dense BI dashboard visuals, perfect geometric shapes, or clean vector icons.
```

## 补充说明

- **主笔**: 黑色马克笔线条，有有机变化，略波浪，不完美
- **底色**: 暖奶油色 `#F5F0E8 ~ #FAF6EE`，干净纯色，不要网格、不要纹理
- **容器**: 柔和圆角矩形，内部有淡粉彩填充，边缘略不规则
- **配色分工**:
  - 薄荷绿: 正向、完成、正确
  - 浅蓝: 信息、流程、数据
  - 暖桃橙: 注意、对比、强调
  - 淡紫: 结构、分类
  - 淡黄: 高亮区域
  - 低饱和但清晰可见，像柔和马克笔画在暖底上
- **角色**: 简约火柴人，大头小身， sparingly 使用辅助叙事
- **文字**: 主标题/标签用干净无衬线体；箭头旁小注释用手写风格
- **箭头**: 细手绘线条 + 小箭头头，不要粗块状箭头
- **禁忌**:
  - 不要纯白背景
  - 不要网格纸背景
  - 不要真实照片感
  - 不要高饱和配色
  - 不要深色背景
  - 不要 3D 渲染和光泽效果
  - 不要几何精确的圆角和直线
  - 不要粗块状箭头
  - 不要密集 BI 看板风格

## 版式倾向

### 对比类图 (A vs B)
- 左右分栏，中间用箭头或分隔线
- 顶部大标题
- 每栏内部简洁，用不同颜色区分两侧
- 底部可加总结金句

### 流程类图
- 3-5 个圆角步骤容器
- 容器之间用手绘箭头连接
- 每个步骤内部留足空白
- 底部金句总结流程要义

### 架构类图
- 分层或分组模块，层级清晰
- 层与层之间用细线连接
- 保持简洁，不要过度装饰
- 火柴人在角落思考或 pointing

### 抽象概念类图
- 中心辐射式布局，核心概念居中
- 周围 3-5 个圆角卡片式子概念
- 用不同粉彩区分不同概念类别
- 底部荧光笔金句收尾
